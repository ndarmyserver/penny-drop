Penny Drop

Instructions

Everyone starts with 10 pennies.

Your goal is to be the first one to get rid of all your pennies.

On your turn, you have to roll at least once.

When you roll:

- If the number spot on the board matching the dice is open, place one of your pennies there.

- If not, pick up all the pennies on the board, and your turn is over.

- The 6 spot is always open to “drop” a penny in.

If you didn’t have to pick up the pennies on your first roll, you can choose to continue rolling or end your turn.
